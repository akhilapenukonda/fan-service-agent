FAN SERVICES KNOWLEDGE BASE — REFUND POLICY
(Grounded in real, publicly posted NFL and team ticketing policies)

Tickets purchased directly from the club (season tickets, group sales, or partial
season plans) are eligible for a full refund, or credit toward a future purchase,
only under these circumstances:
- The game is officially canceled and cannot be rescheduled.
- The game is played under conditions that prohibit fans from attending
  (e.g., closed to the public).
- The game is relocated to a different venue than originally scheduled.

If a game is merely postponed and a new date is announced within 30 days,
existing tickets remain valid for the new date, and a cash refund is not
required (the club may still offer one at its discretion).

No refund is provided if the ticket holder simply cannot attend a game that
is played as scheduled. This applies to season tickets, partial plans, and
single-game tickets purchased directly from the club.

Single-game tickets purchased through licensed resale channels (e.g.,
Ticketmaster, SeatGeek) may have different refund terms set by those
platforms, not the club directly.
