FAN SERVICES KNOWLEDGE BASE — GENERAL FAQ

Season ticket accounts may be forwarded/transferred to another person for
individual games through the club's official digital ticketing platform.

Lost, stolen, or destroyed tickets may be replaced with a seat authorization
pass for that individual game; a fee equal to the face value typically
applies.

Playoff tickets are not included in a standard season ticket package.
Season ticket holders in good standing are typically given the opportunity
to purchase playoff tickets for their same seat location before they go on
public sale, if the team qualifies for and hosts a postseason game.

Tickets are a revocable license, not a guaranteed right of entry — admission
may be refused under certain circumstances (see Conduct policy).
